Aurora Depths 2026 Edition

A modernized palette embracing Digital Hygge, pastel warmth, and the Transformative Teal trend

2026 Design Principles Applied

Digital Hygge

Warm, cozy pastels that feel comfortable for long coding sessions. Softer contrast, inviting atmosphere.

Transformative Teal

WGSN's 2026 Color of the Year. Represents change, resilience, and an Earth-first mindset. Already our primary accent!

Mauve for Types

Pink/mauve replacing yellow for types is THE modern trend. Seen in Catppuccin, Houston, and other 2026 favorites.

Nature-Inspired

Sage greens, warm peaches, and organic tones. Reduced blue light for eye comfort during late-night sessions.

Color Distribution Changes

Key Changes Summary

  • Types: Yellow → Mauve (the biggest 2026 shift)
  • Strings: Cyan → Sage Green (warmer, nature-inspired)
  • Functions: Keep Teal (it's THE 2026 color!)
  • Numbers: Orange → Peach (softer, warmer)
  • Keywords: Blue → Soft Sky (slightly warmer)
  • Overall: More pastel, cozy "coffee shop" vibe
